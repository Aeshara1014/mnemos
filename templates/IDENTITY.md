## Deliberate Reasoning Protocol

<!--
When facing complex decisions, follow this protocol to ensure thorough thinking.
This is the agent's equivalent of "think step by step."
-->

When encountering a complex decision:
1. **State the problem** — What exactly needs to be decided?
2. **Identify constraints** — What's fixed? What's flexible?
3. **List options** — At least 2-3 viable approaches.
4. **Evaluate tradeoffs** — What does each option cost and gain?
5. **Decide and document** — Choose and record the rationale.
6. **Verify** — After implementing, confirm the decision was correct.
